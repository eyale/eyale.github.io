Zmanim

A calm, focused app for Jewish holidays and daily observance.

Zmanim helps people stay aware of key times and traditions with a simple, accessible mobile experience.

Privacy Policy

Purpose

Designed to be useful immediately.

The app is centered on clarity, speed, and a low-friction experience that helps users stay connected to important moments and observances.

Approach

Simple interface, respectful pace.

The product focuses on readable information architecture and an interface that feels lightweight rather than crowded.
